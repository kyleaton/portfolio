About Me

I am a UX Designer and 500 Startups alumni based in Los Angeles with seven years of experience working in design and, since 2013, a focus on user experience and front end development. I get true satisfaction in collecting and digesting data in order to bring a product to its true potential. Currently I am most excited about growth and perfecting the management of a growth machine.

When I’m not in the product world, you can find me surfing, camping or pickling. I’m always interested in new opportunities; feel free to email me and connect with me on LinkedIn.
