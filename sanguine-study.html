Intro to Sanguine

Sanguine is a biotech company that at the time of my employment was focused on improving in-home clinical trials in two ways: Recruit patients with digital marketing and developing technology to aid in collecting samples from patients in their homes.

My time at Sanguine was spent helping to create their mobile phlebotomy app Sangre and improving their patient sign up process.

Role: User Experience Manager

Main Responsibilities:
  • - Wire-framing, user flow charts and lo fi mocks.
  • - Manage a small team of graphic designers.
  • - Communicating functionality to developers.
  • - Front end development (HTML, SCSS, Javascript).
  • - Planning the infrastructure and analyzing analytics.
  • - Planning and designing A/B tests.
  • - Collecting user feedback.
